RWX now running 2.3.2

By Neil De Souza
January 23, 2008

After a good hour RWX has finally been upgraded to WordPress 2.3.2.

I've been putting off actually updating my WP installation for over a year now, but when a snag hit my pages I decided it was finally time to get up with the process.

While I wouldn't call my upgrade a smooth process, it nevertheless is nothing that will faze a newbie. So if you're really thinking of upgrading, just follow the extended upgrade process and it's pretty much a snap.

Cheers again to the WP team and other developers supporting the platform.
